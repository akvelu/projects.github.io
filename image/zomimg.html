Fashion is the armor to survive the reality of everyday life.

"Fashion is part of the daily air and it changes all the time, with all the events. You can even see the approaching of a revolution in clothes. You can see and feel everything in clothes."

You can have anything you want in life if you dress for it.

"I think there is beauty in everything. What 'normal' people perceive as ugly, I can usually see something of beauty in it.Style is something each of us already has, all we need to do is find it."

Clothes mean nothing until someone lives in them.

"You gotta have style. It helps you get down the stairs. It helps you get up in the morning. It’s a way of life. Without it, you’re nobody. I’m not talking about lots of clothes.”

Style is a way to say who you are without having to speak.

"Style is the only thing you can’t buy. It’s not in a shopping bag, a label, or a price tag. It’s something reflected from our soul to the outside world—an emotion.”
